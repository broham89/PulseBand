Specifications

Sizing

Small: 6.2 inches (157.5 mm)
Medium: 6.5 inches (166 mm)
Large 7.1 inches (181 mm)

Sensors & Components

3-axis accelerometers
3-axis gyroscope
EKG reader
Pulse oximetry monitor
Optical heart rate monitor
Vibration motor with haptic feedback
Piezo-style speaker
Bluetooth 4.0 LE

Display

Hi-resolution display
Touch screen

Battery life: Lasts up to 7 days

LED indicator lights
Wi-Fi enabled with optional cellular data
GPS capabilities
Subscription-based emergency service*
